About Project Overwatch

Overwatch is an AI-based project. We aim to provide a fast, efficient and simple way to find the missing culprits. Automated video surveillance is crucial for the security of various sites including airports, train stations, military bases, shopping malls and any other public or private facilities. There have been significant advances in automated visual surveillance systems in recent years but in india we are not having any efficient systems for it. Indeed there are many areas of application, we primarily focus to help cops to find the “Culprits in the Lookout list” much faster.
